# Unicorn Adventure Game

A colorful, kid-friendly platformer made for children ages 4–8.

Play as a brave unicorn: run, jump, avoid gloomy rainy clouds, and face the dragon at the end of the adventure!

---

## How to play (quick)

- Move left and right with the Left and Right arrow keys.
- Press the Up arrow key to jump.
- Avoid gloomy clouds that drop rain.
- Use the Space key to fight the dragon at the end of the level.
- The unicorn has 3 lives — try to keep them all!

---

## Goal

Help the unicorn travel through the level safely, avoid hazards, and defeat the dragon at the end. Have fun collecting imaginary victories and practicing simple timing and movement!

---

## Controls

- Left Arrow ← : Move left  
- Right Arrow → : Move right  
- Up Arrow ↑ : Jump  
- Space : Attack / Fight the dragon

---

## Tips for little players

- Take your time and practice jumping over obstacles.  
- Watch the clouds — if they look gloomy, try to jump or avoid them.  
- If you lose a life, take a deep breath and try again — practice makes perfect!

---

## Tips for parents / guardians

- Supervise younger children while they play.  
- You can help by showing them how to time jumps and how to use the Space key to handle the dragon.  
- Encourage short play sessions for younger kids to avoid fatigue.
